Body Lift

What is a body lift?

Body lift, also called circumferential dermolipectomy, is a major surgical procedure that removes excess skin and fat around the entire waist. This intervention is particularly indicated after massive weight loss (bariatric surgery, diet) that left significant skin laxity.

Body lift combines abdominoplasty with back and buttock lift in a single procedure, providing a global improvement of the silhouette.

Who is body lift indicated for?

Body lift is indicated for patients with:

  • Significant circumferential skin excess after massive weight loss
  • Stable weight for at least 6 months
  • Good general health
  • Realistic expectations about results and scars

The procedure

The procedure lasts 4 to 6 hours and is performed under general anesthesia with several days of hospitalization. The scar is circular around the waist, positioned to be hidden by underwear.

The procedure includes removal of excess skin from the abdomen, back and buttocks, as well as muscle tightening and possible liposuction of the flanks.

Postoperative course

Recovery is significant given the extent of surgery:

  • Hospitalization of 3 to 5 days
  • Drains for several days
  • Compression garment for 6 to 8 weeks
  • Work stoppage of 4 to 6 weeks
  • No sports for 2 to 3 months

Final results are visible after 6 months to 1 year.

Risks

Risks include hematoma, seroma, infection, wound healing problems (especially in former smokers), thrombosis, asymmetry and unsightly scars. Dr. Woussen will inform you in detail during consultation.

Results

Body lift offers dramatic results with a completely transformed silhouette. Skin excess is eliminated, waist is defined, buttocks are lifted. This procedure significantly improves quality of life and self-confidence.
