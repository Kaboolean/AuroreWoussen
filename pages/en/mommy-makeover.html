Mommy Makeover

What is a Mommy Makeover?

Mommy Makeover is a combination of plastic surgery procedures performed in a single operation to restore a woman's pre-pregnancy body. This customized approach addresses the various changes caused by pregnancy and breastfeeding: sagging breasts, excess abdominal skin, separated muscles, localized fat deposits.

This complete procedure allows restoring a harmonious silhouette while limiting the number of surgeries and recovery periods.

What procedures are included?

The Mommy Makeover can combine several procedures according to each patient's needs:

  • Breasts: Breast lift (mastopexy), breast augmentation (implants or lipofilling), breast reduction
  • Abdomen: Abdominoplasty (tummy tuck), liposuction, diastasis repair
  • Silhouette: Liposuction of hips, flanks, thighs

Who is it for?

Mommy Makeover is indicated for women who:

  • Have completed their pregnancies
  • Have stopped breastfeeding for at least 6 months
  • Have a stable weight
  • Are in good general health
  • Have realistic expectations

Consultation

During consultation, Dr. Woussen will perform a complete examination to evaluate the areas to be treated. She will propose a personalized surgical plan adapted to your anatomy, desires and lifestyle. A detailed quote will be provided.

The procedure

The procedure lasts 3 to 5 hours depending on the procedures performed. It is done under general anesthesia with 1 to 3 nights of hospitalization.

Postoperative course

Recovery depends on the procedures performed:

  • Wearing compression garments for 4 to 6 weeks
  • Work stoppage of 2 to 4 weeks
  • No sports for 6 to 8 weeks
  • Final results visible after 3 to 6 months

Risks

Risks are those associated with each procedure performed. Dr. Woussen will explain them in detail during consultation.

Results

The Mommy Makeover offers transformative results, allowing women to regain their pre-pregnancy body, even improved. This procedure has a significant positive impact on self-confidence and quality of life.
