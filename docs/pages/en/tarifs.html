The prices of cosmetic surgery procedures vary according to several factors: the complexity of the procedure, the technique used, the duration of the operation, and hospitalization fees.

During the consultation, Dr. Woussen will provide you with a detailed and personalized quote for the procedure you wish to perform.

Consultation

The initial consultation allows us to evaluate your request, examine your morphology and discuss the different therapeutic options. The consultation fee will be communicated to you when booking an appointment.

Personalized Quote

Each procedure being unique, a personalized quote will be given to you during the consultation. This quote includes:

  • Surgical fees
  • Anesthesia fees
  • Hospitalization fees
  • Implants or materials used if necessary

Financing

Cosmetic surgery is not covered by French Social Security. However, some procedures may be partially reimbursed if they are performed in a medical context (reconstruction after mastectomy, correction of malformation, etc.).

Financing solutions may be offered. Do not hesitate to contact us for more information.
